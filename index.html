Why?

Understanding mental health challenges is the first step towards healing. Anxiety affect millions, causing excessive worry, restlessness, and fatigue. Depression, impacting over 17 million adults annually, brings persistent sadness, loss of interest, and fatigue. Suicide, a coping mechanism for emotional distress, is especially common among adolescents and young adults. It's crucial to know that these conditions stem from complex interactions of genetic, environmental, and psychological factors, and effective treatments are available.
